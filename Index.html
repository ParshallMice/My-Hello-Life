What is Ad Repellant?

Hello User,
      Ad Repellant is a donation system on Hello Life that removes ads from the site. In my opinion, the ads on Hello Life detract from the overall smooth, clean feel of the app. And I don't want to do that to you the user but I, as a college student, need to make money somehow. And I have been working on this app since 2013. Up to this point (Feb, 2018), I have yet to make a dime off of all of the work that I put into this project (Hello Life). So the Ad Repellant button below will help you to contribute to the ongoing development of this app. The MyHelloLife.com domain is going to cost us about $10 per year. So donations are also nessecary for that. If you want the ads and the 'Ad Repellant' button to not display for you, you have to donate at least $5.
Thanks,
